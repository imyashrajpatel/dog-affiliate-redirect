What Does Brain Training for Dogs Include?

The Brain Training for Dogs program encompasses all the essentials for transforming your dog into a well-behaved companion that willingly follows your commands

Module 1 - Preschool

This session will teach you to focus your dog on you no matter what is happening around you. By the end of this module, you'll be able to hold your dog's attention. You'll also be able to target them to switch their attention to something else.

Our dog started looking into our eyes and paying attention to us while we played games like "Target Train," "Magic Little Way," and "Airplane Game."

Module 2 - ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

After your dog has mastered the basics, you can move on to the second module where they learn to follow your commands. This module includes engaging activities such as 'treasure hunt,' 'ball pit,' and the 'muffin game.' These games not only provide exercise but also make learning enjoyable for your dog while helping you manage their energy. If motivating your dog is a challenge, Adrienne recommends using high-value treats.

Module 3 - HIGH SCHOOL

After mastering the elementary level, your dog advances to high school, where they learn the art of calming down and self-control. This module also introduces the basics of scent work, which can be both educational and entertaining.

This level includes activities like 'bobbing for treats,' 'jazz up and settle down,' which teaches your dog how to settle down after being hyped, and 'the bottle game,' which provides mental stimulation along with a bit of exercise.

Module 4 - COLLEGE

From this point, things become more interesting. We began to notice a lot of changes in our dog, and she appears much happier than before. Occasionally, we revisit the activities from the previous module.

This module focuses on teaching your dog how to concentrate on your commands, and it emphasizes patience and calmness. The games included at this level are 'The Magic Carpet Game,' 'The Shell Game,' and 'The Open Sesame Game.

Module 5 - UNIVERSITY

This module introduces more advanced concepts. We particularly enjoyed the hide and seek game, which showcased our dog's intelligence and strengthened our bond with her.

In addition to hide and seek, there are other games such as 'Look at That Game,' which can assist with barking issues, and the 'Hot and Cold Game,' designed to boost your dog's confidence.

Module 6 - GRADUATION

This session will teach you to focus your dog on you no matter what is happening around you. By the end of this module, you'll be able to hold your dog's attention. You'll also be able to target them to switch their attention to something else.
